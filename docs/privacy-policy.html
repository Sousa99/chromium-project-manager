Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025.05.31

Thank you for using Chromium Project Manager (the “Extension”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

By using the Extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All data entered into the Chromium Project Manager extension—such as project names, ticket titles, ticket links, or any other details—is stored locally on your browser. This data is never sent to any external server, and we do not have access to it.

We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information in any way.

All actions performed within the Extension are processed entirely within your browser. The Extension’s purpose is solely to help you organize and manage your work by allowing you to:

  • Create and manage custom "projects"
  • Add and store ticket names and URLs associated with those projects
  • Copy relevant information to your clipboard for development and sprint review purposes

3. Data Storage and Security

All data used by the Extension is stored locally on your device using your browser's extension storage (such as chrome.storage.local). We do not have access to this data.

Your data remains private and secure as long as your device and browser are secure.

We recommend following best practices for browser and device security, such as keeping software up-to-date and using antivirus protection.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share or disclose any data to third parties.

The Extension does not integrate with or rely on any external APIs, services, or cloud platforms.

5. User Accounts and Authentication

The Extension does not require a user account or login of any kind. There is no registration process, and we do not collect usernames, passwords, or email addresses.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the functionality of the Extension changes in a way that affects your privacy. If any future version introduces external services or data collection, we will update this policy and inform users accordingly.

We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

We respect your privacy. We do not collect or transmit any personal data. All your information stays local to your browser and is fully under your control.
